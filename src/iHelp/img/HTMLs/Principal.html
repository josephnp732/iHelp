Dr. Sanjay Jain, Principal

Dr. Sanjay Jain has around three decades of experience across industry and academia, where he has had the roles of researcher, business manager, entrepreneur, consultant and professor.

He has worked at Hewlett Packard where he was responsible for the incubation, development and release of several HP R&D products. The solutions provided were in the area of information technology and communication network management for telecom companies and enterprise customers.

Dr. Jain has represented Hewlett-Packard at several international forums, interacted with Fortune 500 customers as well as with consulting partners such as Deloitte Consulting and Cap Gemini. He has managed global programmes for HP R&D product releases.
